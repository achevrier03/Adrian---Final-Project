A Brief Introduction

Welcome to KlumKast, the site
made for and all about model,
producer and business woman,
Heidi Klum. Essentially being a
virtual biography, this site has a
lot to offer when it comes to
learning more about Heidi Klum.

Heidi Klum's childhood was just like the one of
any other child. She lived in a small town in
Germany, and wasn't extremely wealthy. She was
your average middle-class family child. As years
went by however, her ambitions to work in the
fashion industry grew as she got older. To read
more about her life before fame, check out the
"Childhood" page.

Heidi Klum seems to have
done br everything. From
acting, to modeling, to being
a judge on America's Got
Talent, she's managed to
juggle careers while still
being able to take care of
her family and of herself. To
read more about her many
jobs, check out the "Career"
page.

Heidi's had her fair share of
husbands, while also being the
mother to four kids. We've been
told she's an excellent mother,
and has finally been able to meet
her perfect match. Her
relashionship with her current
husband is just about perfect,
so it seems. To read more about
her family and dating life, check
out the "Relations" page.
